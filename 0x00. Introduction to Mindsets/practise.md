# How to practise growth mindset everyday

1. Start day with writing things I am grateful for, this is to help focus on the positive and keep a positive mindset throughout the day.

2. Setting small daily learning goals

3. Celebrating my successes no matter how small they are.

4. Approaching challenges with open mind and seeing them as opportunities to learning and growth.

5. Learning from criticisim

6. Pesevere in the face of setbacks

7. Be curious

8. Acknowledgeing and overcoming fixed mindset thoughts.

9. Learning from others' successes

10. Stay persistent
