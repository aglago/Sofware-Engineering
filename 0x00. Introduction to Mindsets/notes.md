# Mindset

**What is grit?**
IQ is not the only difference between best and worst students. Doing well in school and in life depends on much more than my ability to learn and quickly.

One characteristic emerged as a significant predictor of success, it was **grit**

**Grit** is passion and perseverance for very long-term goals. It is having stamina and sticking with my future day in day out. Not just for the week, not just for the month but for years and working really hard to make that future a reality.

**Grit** is living life like it is a marathon, not a sprint.

## How to build grit?

**Growth mindset:** The ability to learn is not fixed, but it can change with efforts.

Read and learn about the brain and how it changes and grows in response to challenge, I am much more likely to persevere when I fail, because this will make me believe that failure is a permanent condition
